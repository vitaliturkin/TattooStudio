About Us

Tattooing is an ancient art form that enables modern individuals to express their unique identity. At Tattoomania, located in the heart of Edinburgh, we specialise in creating artistic tattoos that bring your ideas to life. If you want to adorn your body with meaningful artwork, we are the right place for you.
